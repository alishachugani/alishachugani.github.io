How to make pizza for kids

This 15 Minute Pizza Recipe for kids makes a quick lunch or supper the whole family will enjoy.
Ingredients you will need to make one small pizza: 1 piece of pita bread, 2-3 tablespoons of pizza sauce, ½ cup shredded Mozzarella cheese, the toppings you will be adding.

  1. Step 1: Preheat oven to 450º F.

    Before we start making the pizza you should preheat your oven to 450º F.

  2. Step 2: Place pita bread on a baking sheet.

    We will start by placing the pita bread on a baking sheet or the trey you will use to bake the pizza.

  3. Step 3: Spread pizza sauce.

    Next we will spread pizza sauce on top of the pita bread.

  4. Step 4: Add cheese and your favorite toppings

    After we have spread the pizza sauce we will continue by adding the shredded cheese and the toppings you want in the pizza.

  5. Step 5: Place pizza in oven.

    Once you have added all your toppings, place the pizza in the oven for about 10 minutes or until the cheese has completely melted and become a bit bubbly but not browned

  6. Step 6: Remove from oven.

    Remove the pizza from the oven and slice or serve whole.
    Enjoy!
