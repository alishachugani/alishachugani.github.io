Throughout my childhood, I have taken several different art related clases including, drawings and painting techniques.I began sculpture classes in Taller Quimera where I took an interest to pursue kiln glass sculptures.

On July 2013, I completed various courses conducted by Bullseye Glass Co. in Portland, Oregon, mostly in kiln glass and painting with glass.

I have participated in the first biennial of the South in Panama 2013, Throughout these last years, I have had presence in many important collective art exhibitions in Panama, Bogota Miami and in New York.

On december 6th, 2017, had an Individual showing of kiln formed and kiln glass sculptures in Panama.

My art is oriented to an inherited cultural remembrance. My work represents my individual spiritual search where I strive to connect with myself and the universe.

I like to visualize the outcome of the final piece, but there is always a surprise element at the end of the firing, where the kilm works its magic in the blending of the glass.
